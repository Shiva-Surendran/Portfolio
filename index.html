Care Connect App

Created an app for monitoring isolated individuals and senior citizens to ensure their wellbeing. CareConnect checks the inactivity of the beneficiary and if there is a discrepancy it will inform the caretaker. Also, the app incorporates scream detection, fall detection, emergency call and SMS feature, and pill reminder. Technologies: Flutter

URLcomments
